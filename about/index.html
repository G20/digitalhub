About

The G20 Repository of Digital Policies is a web platform that gathers digitalization policies undertaken by G20 countries to maximize the benefits and address the challenges of the digital economy.

The areas covered are:
  • Skills for the future of work
  • Digital inclusion
  • Digital infrastructure
  • Emerging technologies
  • Digital government
  • MSMEs and entrepreneurship

In order to contribute to the objective of strengthening a digital agenda for development agreed by the G20 Digital Economy Task Force during the Argentine Presidency, this platform was developed to promote the exchange of experiences and create a community of practice.

The target audience comprises policymakers, academia, MSMEs, entrepreneurs, and international and civil society organizations. The platform aims to provide to different stakeholders the opportunity to quickly understand and access the diverse set of policy initiatives that are in place regarding digitalization as well as identify the institutions and teams responsible for them.
